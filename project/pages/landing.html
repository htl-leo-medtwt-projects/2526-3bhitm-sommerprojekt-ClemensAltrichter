How does it work?

You know the feeling everyone's ready for a movie night, but half an hour later you're still scrolling and debating. That's exactly why we built Cine Match.

Create your personal movie list, start a Watch Party with friends, and swipe through everyone's picks together. Like what you see, skip what you don't. At the end, the most liked film wins

no endless scrolling.




Just swipe, vote, and watch.
